Welcome to Sciencethics

Sciencethics serve as a platform for researchers and healthcare professionals to share their findings, studies, and advancements in the field of medicine. They help disseminate new information, promote evidence-based practices, and contribute to the overall knowledge and understanding of various medical conditions and treatments.

Researchers can help ensure that the information presented is accurate and up-to-date. Additionally, researchers can provide valuable insights and expertise in a specific field of study. Their expertise can also provide valuable insights and perspectives on complex medical topics, contributing to the overall quality of the publication.

Directive Publications is an open access publisher that focuses on providing high publication ethics. They have established policies to ensure the integrity of their publications and adhere to the Code of Conduct and Best Practice Guidelines. Directive Publications aims to follow the ethical guidelines developed by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) as the best practice.

All the journals published by Directive Publications are freely accessible to readers worldwide without any subscription charges. Authors can reuse or redistribute articles from this journal as long as the original work is correctly cited.
