New Impact Factor Released for 2025

December 18, 2025 | Journal Update

Directive Publications is pleased to announce the release of the latest Impact Factor results for 2025. Several of our clinical and scientific journals have demonstrated significant growth in citation metrics, reflecting increased global engagement and research visibility.

The reported data shows an average increase of over 20% in citation rates across multiple disciplines, including medical sciences, environmental studies, and interdisciplinary research. This achievement underscores the commitment of our editorial boards, reviewers, and authors to maintaining high academic standards.

Impact Factor remains one of the most recognized indicators of journal performance, and while it is not the sole measure of quality, it provides valuable insight into the influence and reach of published research. Directive Publications continues to support transparent and ethical publishing practices aligned with international standards.

We extend our gratitude to all contributors and invite researchers worldwide to submit their latest findings to our journals as we continue advancing open-access scientific knowledge.
